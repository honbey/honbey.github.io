Set spawner of skeleton

setblock ~ ~1 ~ minecraft:mob_spawner 0 replace{spawnData:{id:"minecraft:skeleton"},SpawnCount:1,SpawnRange:10,Delay:20,MinSpawnDelay:200,MaxSpawnDelay:600,RequiredPlayerRange:10,MaxNearbyEntities:2}

Give a player a custom diamond sword

/give @a minecraft:diamond_sword{display:{Name:"{"text":"name","color":"color_id","strikethrough":"
true","bold":"true","underlined":"true"}"}} 1
